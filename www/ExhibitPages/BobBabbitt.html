Bob Babbitt has played bass on “Signed, Sealed, Delivered I’m Yours” by Stevie Wonder, “War” by Edwin Starr, “The Tears of a Clown” by Smokey Robinson & the Miracles. He has participated in hundreds of other hits, including “Mercy Mercy Me (The Ecology)” and “Inner City Blues” by Marvin Gaye, and “Ball of Confusion (That’s What the World Is Today)” by The Temptations.
